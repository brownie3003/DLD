Digital Learning Day

This is a live document and is subject to change

The Digital Learning Day is intended to introduce Technology in Business fast streamers to modern coding and development practice. Participants will learn the basics of front end technologies such as HTML/CSS and programming languages with a focus on JavaScript. The day will be hands on with participants coding throughout the day.

This will be the first Digital Learning Day that we will run. This will be our Alpha, you will be our test group. We will be asking for a lot of feed back and we hope you won't be disappointed if you don't get this day, but we didn't have £750 per person.

Date: Friday 26th of July

Location: Manchester Tech Hub
